About

My name is Sam Kwan and I am a freelance photographer based in Auckland, New Zealand.

I shoot events, weddings, portraiture - anything involving people.

My wedding photography style is primarily documentary or reportage style. I aim to capture images that together paint the story of a couple's special day.

Capturing those intimate and powerful moments that last only for an instant is what inspires me when shooting weddings.

Please get in contact with me if you need a photographer for events, weddings or portraits.
